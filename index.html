ABOUT ME

Raised in an environment where music, art and the outdoors was an everyday occurrence has given me a life full of musical expression and adventure. Although I am deeply moved by music, nothing gives me the feels like mother nature which led me to attaining my B.S. in Ecology. Before my pursuit of web development I was employed as an Organic Chemistry Technician in Washington state. I provided state and city agencies along with local businesses an assurance that their water and soils were safe for consumption and agricultural production. A serious shoulder injury forced me to leave my job and for 6 months after surgery I spent my time learning to code. I quickly became obsessed. Here was a medium that allowed me to be involved in all of the things I find important: Music, Education and Environmental Science. There really is no limit to what I can create and solve with programming and that is exciting!
