PROJECTS

Open-Mic

Open-Mic is a digital stage to post your video musical performances and listen to the sounds of your fellow musicians. Hop up on stage and share or kick back with your favorite beverage and listen to the latest songs from your favorite performers. With SoundCloud and other apps becoming overly complicated, Open-Mic is here for a simple and enjoyable interface to share your performances.

Tech: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, ExpressJS, NodeJS, PostgreSQL, Mocha, Chai, Supertest, Jest

Penny Thoughts

Penny Thoughts is an app that allows users to share and view positive, motivational statements. With today's quarantine keeping people at home, Penny Thoughts helps bring positivity to users by showing them positive statements. Sign up and begin reaping the benefits of positivity. A.I. filter made using TensorFlow.js and a pre-built Toxicity model.

Spaced-Repetition

Spaced Repetition is a language learning application. Practice learning a language with the spaced repetition revision technique. This app is for those wanting to learn the Spanish language. Other apps tend to give you the same words time and time again. Our algorithms will make sure you practice the words and phrases that you have answered incorrectly at a higher frequency.

Tech: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, NodeJS, ExpressJS, PostgreSQL, Cypress, Mocha
